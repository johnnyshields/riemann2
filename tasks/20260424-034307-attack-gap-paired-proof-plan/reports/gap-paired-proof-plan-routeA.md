# gap-paired-proof-plan-routeA

## Claim

The cleanest theorem-proof plan for the paired Dirichlet source-plus-slot milestone is a localized two-step package:

1. a compact-interval paired source-package theorem for
   `\Phi_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}(s)=\Xi_\chi(2s-1)/\Xi_\chi(2s)` with
   `\Xi_\chi(s)=\Lambda(s,\chi)\Lambda(s,\bar\chi)`;
2. an exact paired local-slot theorem showing that the resulting
   `S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}(m)=q_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}(m)-B_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}(m)` is exactly the coefficient of the paired first-order value-channel derivative in
   `\Delta_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}=S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}A_{\mathrm{val},\chi}^{\mathrm{pair}}+R_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`.

The first safe stop-point is weaker: a compact-interval paired source-package blueprint with the theorem body and patch order fixed, while exact paired slot realization, unified background closure, and compact-interval convergence/multiplicity are still marked missing.

`heuristic`

## Evidence

### Theorem endpoint

The exact theorem endpoint should be stated as follows.

For primitive nonprincipal complex `\chi` in the paired setting, define the strip-edge quotient
`\Phi_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}(s)=\Xi_\chi(2s-1)/\Xi_\chi(2s)` with
`\Xi_\chi(s)=\Lambda(s,\chi)\Lambda(s,\bar\chi)`.
Assume the local paired deformation has the symmetric normal form and corrected holomorphic whitening needed to define the paired first-order value channel. Then on compact intervals one has a unified decomposition
`q_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}(m)=B_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}(m)+S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}(m)`,
and the exact paired local identity
`\Delta_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}=S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}A_{\mathrm{val},\chi}^{\mathrm{pair}}+R_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`.

This is the correct milestone because it reaches the manuscript-style exact `S(m)` slot and no further. It does not include remainder dominance, odd/transverse realization, boundary theorems, or any ERH/GRH consequence.

### Ordered proof plan

1. Fix the paired source object and theorem scope.
State the target only for the paired quotient built from `\Xi_\chi`; keep the scope as `from current scope alone` and `first non-zeta exact-source realization`.

2. State the compact-interval source theorem body upstairs.
Isolate a source-package theorem on compact intervals for `q_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`, with a single unified background term `B_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}` absorbing conductor/scaling, gamma-derivative, and trivial-zero or pole corrections.

3. Separate theorem body from bookkeeping burdens.
Inside the theorem body, only claim the decomposition
`q_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}=B_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}+S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`.
Keep full background identification, convergence regularization, and multiplicity conventions as explicit sub-burdens to be discharged in the source-package proof.

4. Add the minimal local admissibility package.
Import, as hypotheses rather than conclusions, the symmetric local normal form, microscopic holomorphy, same-point positivity/nondegeneracy, well-defined inverse-square-root whitening, and existence of the corrected paired first-order value-channel derivative.

5. Prove well-posedness of the slot identity.
Use Step 4 only to make the exact identity
`\Delta_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}=S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}A_{\mathrm{val},\chi}^{\mathrm{pair}}+R_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`
mathematically meaningful. Do not promote denominator comparability to a co-equal theorem endpoint.

6. Identify the paired `S(m)` slot exactly.
Show that the `S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}` produced by the source package is exactly the coefficient of `A_{\mathrm{val},\chi}^{\mathrm{pair}}` in the corrected local expansion. This is the real milestone closure.

7. Stop.
Record everything else as downstream. In particular, do not fold in remainder dominance, corrected-whitening transport estimates, odd/transverse realization, or boundary estimates.

### Theorem body vs hypothesis package vs deferred

Theorem body:

- paired strip-edge quotient built from `\Xi_\chi`;
- compact-interval paired source decomposition `q_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}=B_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}+S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`;
- exact paired slot identity `\Delta_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}=S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}A_{\mathrm{val},\chi}^{\mathrm{pair}}+R_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`.

Hypothesis package:

- primitive nonprincipal complex-character scope;
- symmetric local normal form for the paired deformation;
- microscopic holomorphy;
- same-point positivity/nondegeneracy;
- holomorphic inverse-square-root whitening admissibility;
- existence of the corrected paired first-order value-channel derivative.

Deferred:

- remainder dominance;
- quantitative corrected-whitening transport;
- denominator comparability as a standalone theorem endpoint;
- odd/transverse realization;
- boundary estimates;
- calibration uniformity;
- any ERH/GRH contradiction or endgame claim.

### Proved / conditional / missing

Proved:

- the theorem-facing target should use the paired quotient `\Phi_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`, not just the raw paired product;
- the clean milestone shape is source-plus-slot, not source alone;
- the safe patch order is compact-interval source package first, exact slot identification second;
- the slot theorem needs an explicit local admissibility package;
- same-point positivity/nondegeneracy should be named explicitly and not buried under generic denominator holomorphy language;
- the milestone stops before remainder dominance, odd/transverse work, and boundary control.

Conditional on imported local package:

- once the symmetric local normal form and corrected whitening admissibility are in place, the exact identity
  `\Delta_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}=S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}A_{\mathrm{val},\chi}^{\mathrm{pair}}+R_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`
  is a well-posed theorem target;
- once the compact-interval source package is proved, positivity of the upstairs one-zero strip-edge kernel is automatic in the paired source object.

Missing:

- a proved compact-interval paired source theorem with unified `B_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}`;
- exact source-to-slot identification showing that this `S_\chi^{\mathrm{pair}}` is the coefficient of the paired value-channel derivative;
- full compact-interval background/convergence/multiplicity closure;
- a standalone cross-family realization of the same-point positivity package;
- all downstream dominance and boundary theorems.

### Honest verdict

There is a clean theorem-proof plan, but not a clean theorem proof. The safest endpoint is a localized paired source-plus-slot theorem with explicit local admissibility hypotheses. The first safe stop-point is earlier: fix the compact-interval paired source-package blueprint and the exact slot target, while leaving slot closure and the bookkeeping burdens openly missing.
