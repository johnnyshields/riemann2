`Lambda_F(2s-1) / Lambda_F(2s)`

derivative has the same strip-edge kernel shape as in zeta.

- the positivity of that one-zero kernel once the zero lies in the strip.

## What is still family-specific

- the source theorem itself;
- background identification;
- convergence / regularization;
- the local realization that puts the resulting scalar into the manuscript's
  exact `S(m)` slot.
