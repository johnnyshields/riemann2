## Strongest objection

The compact-source note still leaves unified background, convergence, and multiplicity open, and the slot note requires nontrivial local admissibility not delivered by the upstairs source theorem alone. So the sentence is safe only if it says `2-step theorem-facing patch`, `from current scope alone`, and explicitly defers all downstream remainder and odd-package work.
