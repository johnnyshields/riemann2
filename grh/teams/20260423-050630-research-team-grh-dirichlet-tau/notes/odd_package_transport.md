# Odd Package Transport Note

Date: 2026-04-23
Focused attack: `tasks/20260423-135020-attack-gap-odd-package-transport/`

## Main conclusion

The odd/transverse package is an independent theorem target.

What is universal is narrow:

- once a family already provides a chosen real odd holomorphic transverse scalar
  with microscopic boundary control, the odd-expansion, Cauchy coefficient
  bounds, and `N`-point projector/cancellation machinery are already in place.

What is not universal from local scope alone:

- existence of that real odd channel;
- its symmetry mechanism after correction/whitening;
- the concrete boundary scale used in the manuscript.

## Strongest safe roadmap sentence

`After exact scalar-slot identification and value-channel remainder dominance,
the next independent bottleneck is a family-specific corrected transverse
realization-and-boundary theorem.`

## Smallest missing family theorem

The minimal extra theorem is:

1. produce the corrected odd holomorphic transverse scalar in the chosen
   family/channel;
2. prove the microscopic denominator/whitening control needed to define it;
3. prove the boundary estimate on `|s| = rho_0 / Q`.

After that, coefficient bounds are just Cauchy and the `N`-point projector is
already universal.

## Scoped warning

Do not say `the odd package transports automatically after source theorem plus
positive S`.

Safe replacement:

`the odd-germ/projector algebra transports once the family supplies the realized
odd scalar and its boundary control; that realized odd scalar remains a separate
theorem target.`
