# gap-closer-gauge

- **Claim**: A quantum-side analogue of the paper's jet-Gramian exists only after two repairs. The naive matrix built from projected coordinate derivatives and then whitened is not a gauge-invariant, reparameterization-covariant matrix object. What is genuinely invariant is narrower: same-point Gram data of first horizontal derivatives; more generally the osculating jet subspace generated by covariant derivatives; and, after parallel transport between parameters, the singular values/principal angles between the corresponding orthonormal jet frames.
- **Status**: proved
- **Evidence**: Proved: for a normalized state \(|\psi(\lambda)\rangle\) with \(P(\lambda)=I-|\psi\rangle\langle\psi|\), the first projected jet \(j_1:=P\partial_\lambda\psi\) transforms covariantly under phase gauge, so same-point quantities such as \(\langle j_1,j_1\rangle\) are gauge invariant. Proved: the second projected coordinate jet \(j_2:=P\partial_\lambda^2\psi\) does not transform covariantly: \(j_2\mapsto e^{i\theta}(j_2+2i\theta'j_1)\). Higher jets mix with lower jets by an upper-triangular gauge-change law. Proved: mixed overlaps between two parameters are not gauge invariant even at first order unless one inserts parallel transport. Proved: under reparameterization, higher jets mix lower ones by the chain rule. Proved: whitening is not invariant under general non-orthogonal triangular jet-coordinate changes, so a raw whitened matrix depends on the chosen jet coordinates. Conditional: a clean repaired object is the transported overlap of orthonormal jet frames built from horizontal covariant derivatives in an intrinsic parameter, with invariant content given by singular values/principal angles. Missing: a fully explicit higher-jet covariant derivative convention for all orders; a proof that the chosen intrinsic parameter is globally available and nondegenerate on intended families; worked examples showing nontrivial information beyond ordinary QGT/Fubini-Study data.
- **Exact refs**: `paper/proof_of_rh.tex:114`; `paper/proof_of_rh.tex:171-215`; `paper/proof_of_rh.tex:226-259`; `quantum/findings.md:24-37`; `quantum/20260423-043347-research-team-jet-gram-qm/notes/coordinator-brief.md:21-25,34-36`.
- **Dependencies**: Standard ray-space quantum geometry: normalized states modulo phase, projector \(P=I-|\psi\rangle\langle\psi|\), Berry connection / parallel transport, Fubini-Study metric. Basic chain-rule and gauge-transformation calculus for higher derivatives. Linear algebra fact that whitening is canonical only up to orthogonal/unitary frame changes, not arbitrary congruences.
- **Strongest objection**: The repair weakens the analogy. The paper's object is a concrete whitened matrix with explicit entries, while the gauge- and reparameterization-safe quantum replacement naturally collapses to a unitary orbit or to singular values/principal angles between jet subspaces. Without extra structure fixing a canonical frame and transport convention, there is no honest matrix-valued invariant matching the paper’s whitened blocks entry-by-entry.
- **Needed for promotion**: Define higher quantum jets with an explicit covariant derivative convention. Insert Berry parallel transport in every mixed two-point block. Replace raw whitening as the invariant by singular values/principal angles or else justify an additional canonical frame choice. Work one toy family end-to-end to confirm nontriviality and independence of gauge and reparameterization.
