# Synthesis 2

The focused attack produced a tighter core than the first research-team cycle.

## What now survives cleanly

1. The paper-side object is best described in this track as a **whitened local
   jet cross-Gram block**.
2. The exact first-order quantum bridge is the same-point horizontal-derivative
   Gram matrix
   \[
   Q_{ab}=\langle D_a\psi\mid D_b\psi\rangle,
   \]
   whose real part is the Fubini-Study metric and whose imaginary part gives
   Berry curvature.
3. The harmonic-oscillator variable-frequency family is the best current small
   stress test for nonconstant same-point blocks and bilateral whitening.

## What still fails

- No canonical matrix-valued two-point invariant is justified.
- Higher jets still leak under gauge and reparameterization unless extra
  structure is imposed.
- The oscillator asymmetry is coordinate-sensitive.

## Drafting rule

- Any future `quantum/` paper draft should be written around the first-order
  proposition and the subspace-level invariant viewpoint.
- Matrix-valued two-point constructions must be marked as transported-frame
  objects with extra choices, not as canonical geometry.
